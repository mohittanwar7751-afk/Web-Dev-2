Ques-What is hoisting?

Ans-The variable declaration and function declarationwill move to to the top of the scope.
Hoisting will be applicable for var variable only not for let and const.


Ques-What is TDZ(temporal dead zone)?

Ans-When we declare the variable with let and const without its proper initialization then the variable lies in temporal dead zone until its proper initialization.
